Sample Chapter: CAF Business Perspective Implementation

Overview

The Business Perspective of the AWS Cloud Adoption Framework focuses on ensuring that your cloud investments accelerate your digital transformation ambitions and business outcomes. This chapter provides a comprehensive guide to implementing the Business Perspective across all its foundational capabilities.

Key Capabilities

Strategy Management

Align cloud strategy with business strategy and establish clear success metrics

Portfolio Management

Prioritize cloud investments based on business value and strategic alignment

Innovation Management

Establish processes to drive innovation through cloud capabilities

Product Management

Apply product thinking to cloud services and solutions

Implementation Steps

  1. Assess Current State: Evaluate existing business alignment and cloud readiness
  2. Define Target State: Establish clear business outcomes and success criteria
  3. Gap Analysis: Identify gaps between current and target states
  4. Roadmap Development: Create prioritized implementation roadmap
  5. Execution Planning: Develop detailed execution plans with timelines
  6. Success Measurement: Implement metrics and monitoring systems

Note: This is just a sample from the complete 200+ page implementation guide. The full guide includes detailed worksheets, templates, and step-by-step instructions for every aspect of framework implementation.
