Regional Healthcare Network: $12M Annual Savings Through Strategic Cloud Migration

Industry: Healthcare
Company Size: 15 Hospitals, 50,000 Employees
Project Duration: 6 Months
Investment: $2.5M

Executive Summary

A regional healthcare network with 15 hospitals and 50,000 employees was struggling with aging infrastructure, compliance challenges, and escalating IT costs. CloudNestle designed and implemented a comprehensive AWS migration strategy that achieved 60% cost reduction, 99.99% uptime, and full HIPAA compliance while improving patient care delivery.

The Challenge

Legacy Infrastructure Costs

$20M annual infrastructure costs with aging hardware requiring constant maintenance and frequent failures causing patient care disruptions.

HIPAA Compliance Gaps

Inconsistent security controls across facilities with manual audit processes and potential compliance violations risking $50M+ in fines.

Scalability Limitations

Unable to handle patient volume spikes during flu seasons or emergencies, with systems frequently overloaded.

Data Silos

Patient data scattered across 15 different systems with no unified view, impacting care coordination and clinical decision-making.

Our Solution

Phase 1: Assessment & Planning (Month 1)

  • Comprehensive Well-Architected Review across all 15 facilities
  • HIPAA compliance gap analysis and remediation planning
  • Application portfolio assessment and migration prioritization
  • Cost optimization analysis and ROI projections

Phase 2: Foundation & Security (Months 2-3)

  • Multi-account AWS architecture with centralized security controls
  • HIPAA-compliant landing zone with automated compliance monitoring
  • Identity and access management with role-based permissions
  • Network segmentation and encryption at rest and in transit

Phase 3: Application Migration (Months 4-5)

  • Electronic Health Records (EHR) system migration to RDS Aurora
  • Medical imaging systems moved to S3 with lifecycle policies
  • Patient portal modernization using serverless architecture
  • Real-time data integration with EventBridge and Lambda

Phase 4: Optimization & Training (Month 6)

  • Performance tuning and cost optimization implementation
  • Disaster recovery testing and business continuity validation
  • Staff training and knowledge transfer programs
  • Ongoing support and monitoring setup
