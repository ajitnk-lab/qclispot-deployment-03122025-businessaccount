What is AWS Cloud Adoption Framework?

A comprehensive approach to cloud transformation that addresses all aspects of organizational change

The AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (CAF) provides guidance to help organizations design and travel an accelerated path to successful cloud adoption. CAF organizes guidance into six areas of focus, called Perspectives, which address distinct responsibilities.

Why Choose CAF?

Structured Approach

Proven methodology used by thousands of organizations worldwide

Stakeholder Alignment

Ensures all stakeholders are aligned on transformation goals

Measurable Results

Clear metrics and KPIs to track transformation progress

Risk Mitigation

Identifies and addresses potential risks before they become issues
