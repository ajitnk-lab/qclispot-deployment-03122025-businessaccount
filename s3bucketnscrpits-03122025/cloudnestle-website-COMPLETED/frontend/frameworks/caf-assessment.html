What is AWS Cloud Adoption Framework?

The AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (CAF) is a comprehensive methodology that helps organizations design and travel an accelerated path to successful cloud transformation. It organizes guidance into six areas of focus called Perspectives.

Accelerated Transformation

Reduce cloud adoption timeline by 30-50% with proven methodologies and best practices.

Risk Mitigation

Identify and address potential challenges before they impact your transformation journey.

Stakeholder Alignment

Ensure all stakeholders are aligned on cloud strategy, goals, and implementation approach.

Measurable Outcomes

Define clear success metrics and track progress throughout your cloud transformation.

Six Perspectives of Cloud Transformation

The CAF organizes guidance into six perspectives, each focusing on distinct responsibilities and stakeholders. Our expert consultants evaluate your organization across all perspectives.

Business Perspective

Business Leaders, Finance, Strategy

Ensures cloud investments accelerate business transformation and deliver measurable business outcomes.

Key Capabilities:

  • Strategy Management
  • Portfolio Management
  • Innovation Management
  • Product Management
  • Strategic Partnership
  • Data Monetization
  • Business Insight
  • Data Science
Typical Outcomes: Reduced time-to-market, increased revenue, improved customer satisfaction

People Perspective

HR, Training, Change Management

Supports development of organization-wide change management strategy for successful cloud adoption.

Key Capabilities:

  • Transformation Leadership
  • Cloud Fluency
  • Change Acceleration
  • Culture Evolution
  • Organizational Design
  • Workforce Transformation
  • Organizational Alignment
  • Modern HR
Typical Outcomes: Improved employee engagement, reduced change resistance, accelerated skill development

Governance Perspective

CIO, Program Managers, Enterprise Architects

Focuses on skills and processes to align IT strategy with business strategy and maximize business value.

Key Capabilities:

  • Program Management
  • Project Management
  • Business Analysis
  • Portfolio Management
  • Data Governance
  • Data Curation
  • IT Governance
  • Application Portfolio Management
Typical Outcomes: Improved IT governance, better risk management, optimized cloud investments

Platform Perspective

CTO, IT Managers, Solution Architects

Includes principles and patterns for implementing new solutions and migrating on-premises workloads to the cloud.

Key Capabilities:

  • Platform Architecture
  • Data Engineering
  • Data Architecture
  • Provisioning and Orchestration
  • Modern Application Development
  • Continuous Integration/Deployment
  • System and Solution Architecture
  • Machine Learning
Typical Outcomes: Improved system performance, reduced infrastructure costs, enhanced scalability

Security Perspective

CISO, Security Architects, Compliance

Ensures the organization meets security objectives for visibility, auditability, control, and agility.

Key Capabilities:

  • Security Governance
  • Security Assurance
  • Identity and Access Management
  • Threat Detection
  • Vulnerability Management
  • Infrastructure Protection
  • Data Protection
  • Application Security
Typical Outcomes: Enhanced security posture, improved compliance, reduced security incidents

Operations Perspective

IT Operations, Support Teams, Service Managers

Helps enable, run, use, operate, and recover IT workloads to levels agreed upon with business stakeholders.

Key Capabilities:

  • Observability
  • Event Management
  • Incident and Problem Management
  • Change and Release Management
  • Performance and Capacity Management
  • Configuration Management
  • Patch Management
  • Availability and Continuity Management
Typical Outcomes: Improved operational efficiency, reduced downtime, enhanced service quality
