Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our pricing models and terms

How do you determine pricing for custom projects?

+

We base pricing on project scope, complexity, timeline, and expected outcomes. We provide detailed estimates after an initial consultation and requirements analysis. All pricing is transparent with no hidden costs.

What payment terms do you offer?

+

We offer flexible payment terms including milestone-based payments, monthly plans, and success-based pricing. For startups, we also offer equity partnerships and deferred payment options.

Do you offer discounts for multi-year contracts?

+

Yes, we provide significant discounts for multi-year commitments, typically 15-30% savings. Enterprise clients can receive additional volume discounts and strategic partnership benefits.

What's included in your outcome guarantees?

+

Our guarantees include timeline commitments, performance targets, cost savings achievements, and security compliance. If we don't meet agreed outcomes, we provide remediation at no cost or refund your investment.

Can pricing be adjusted based on actual usage or results?

+

Yes, we offer success-based and usage-based pricing models where costs are tied to actual results achieved or resources consumed. This aligns our success with yours.
