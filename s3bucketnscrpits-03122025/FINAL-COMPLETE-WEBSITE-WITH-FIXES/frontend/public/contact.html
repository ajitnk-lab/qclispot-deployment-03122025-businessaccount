Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process

What happens during the free consultation?

During your free 30-minute consultation, our AWS experts will discuss your current cloud challenges, understand your business objectives, and provide initial recommendations. We'll also explain how our framework-driven approach can benefit your organization and outline potential next steps.

How quickly can you start a project?

We can typically begin assessments within 1-2 weeks of project approval. For implementation projects, the timeline depends on scope and complexity, but our pre-built solutions and accelerators can reduce typical deployment times by 60-70%.

Do you work with companies outside India?

Yes, we serve clients globally. Our team is experienced in working across different time zones and can accommodate various regional compliance requirements. We have successfully delivered projects for clients in North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific.

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in Education (FERPA compliance, EdTech solutions), Retail (e-commerce, omnichannel), and SMB sectors. However, our framework-driven approach and AWS expertise allow us to serve clients across various industries effectively.

What are your service guarantees?

We offer comprehensive guarantees including timeline guarantees (go-live on time or money back), performance guarantees (achieve target metrics or free remediation), and cost savings guarantees (minimum 20% reduction or we pay the difference).

How do you ensure data security and confidentiality?

We follow strict security protocols including signed NDAs, secure communication channels, and compliance with industry standards. Our team is trained in data protection best practices and we can work within your existing security frameworks.
